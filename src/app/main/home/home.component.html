Welcome to our Angular application dedicated to user and job application management. With this platform, you can explore and interact with the following features:

Registration/Login Form:

  • Register by providing your username, password, first name, last name, phone number, and select your user type (recruiter or employee).
  • Log in easily with your username and password.

User Management (CRUD):

  • Create new users.
  • View the list of existing users.
  • Update user information.
  • Delete unnecessary users.

Job Application Management (CRUD):

  • Submit a job application.
  • Track the progress of your application (in-progress state, treated application, etc.).
  • Explore different stages of the application process.

Multi-Criteria User Search:

  • The administrator can perform advanced searches on users using multiple criteria.

Multi-Criteria Job Application Search:

  • The administrator can perform advanced searches on job applications using various criteria.

Modification of Application Status:

  • The administrator has the power to modify the status of job applications to ensure accurate tracking of the process.

We are delighted to welcome you to this interactive experience showcasing the power and flexibility of Angular for creating dynamic and intuitive web applications. Feel free to explore all the features and provide us with your feedback. Enjoy your usage!
